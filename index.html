BMI Range

BMI (Body Mass Index) is an important measurement that can help identify potential health risks such as type 2 diabetes, high cholesterol, high blood pressure, obstructive sleep apnea, fatty liver, osteoarthritis and even certain cancers. It is a calculation used to determine your level of body fat and overall fitness which can help a doctor determine your risk of developing chronic diseases.

Precise Nutrition

Precise Nutrition, also referred to as personalized nutrition, focuses on the individual rather than groups of people. Many research studies on nutrition and types of diets provide useful information for communities and the general population.
